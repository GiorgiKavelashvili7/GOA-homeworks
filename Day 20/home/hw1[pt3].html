About us

More detailed description:

GOA stands for Goal-Oriented academy. The founder of GOA is Mr. Nika Keshelava. The mission of GOA is to mainly teach programming to kids and also teach graphical desingning, english and math. But mostly GOA is focused on having a fun, smart and discipled community. In GOA there are different ranks: [Headmaster] -> [Mentor] -> [Mentor controller] -> [Squad leader controller] -> [Squad leader] -> [Mini squad leader controller] -> [Github controller] -> [Mini squad leader] -> [Student]. Also there are different titles for being the best student in your group. And different grading systems like aura points and etcetura.
