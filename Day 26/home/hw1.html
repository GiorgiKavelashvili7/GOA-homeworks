Football:

Football is a team sport which is played on a grass stadium. It is played in teams of two, each team consisting of 11 players. The goal of the game is to score the most goals. You can score by kicking a ball into your opponent's goal. There are defenders, attackers, Goal-keepers, Referees and other contestants in this game. Also there are special events or rules like the offside, penalty and other events.

Basketball:

Basketball is a team sport which is played in an arena. It is played in teams of two, each team consisting of 5 players. The goal of the game is to score the most points. You can score by throwing a ball into the opponents backboard. There are defenders attackers and special events like fouls, and etectura.
